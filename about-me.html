Biography

I was born on Staten Island, New York on September 21, 2004. My parents are both Jewish immigrants from the former USSR. I was raised in Brooklyn, NY and lived in the neighborhood of Sheepshead Bay for my entire.

Education

I attended James Madison High School in Brooklyn, NY and graduated in 2022. I was a part of the school's honorable Law program and took electives throughout my high school journey specializing in several areas of the law and social issues. However, I later decided not to pursue law while in university. After graduation, I began to attend Stony Brook University where I am currently undeclared, but have applied to change my major to Information Systems as of 2023, with an end goal of changing my major to Computer Science with a double major in Applied Mathematics and Statistics.
